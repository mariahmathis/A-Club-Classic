About the Tournament

The A-Club Classic is an annual golf tournament hosted by the A-Club Alumni Association. All skill levels are welcome — it’s a great day of golf, camaraderie, and giving back.

Proceeds support A-Club programs and initiatives. Entry includes greens fees, cart, swag bag, and post-round dinner.
